INSIGHT 01
Contract Type Is the #1 Risk Signal
Month-to-month customers churn at 42.7% vs just 2.8% for two-year contracts. Converting customers to annual plans is the single highest-ROI retention move.
INSIGHT 02
High Monthly Charges Amplify Churn
Customers paying >$70/month churn at nearly double the rate of those paying <$30/month. Targeted discount offers for high-billing customers can reduce churn significantly.
INSIGHT 03
New Customers Are the Most Vulnerable
Churn is heavily concentrated in customers with <12 months tenure. A structured 90-day onboarding program with check-ins can improve early retention.
INSIGHT 04
Fiber Optic = Higher Churn Risk
Fiber optic internet subscribers churn at higher rates than DSL or no-internet customers — likely due to price sensitivity and competition from rival ISPs.
